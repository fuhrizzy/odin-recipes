Chicken soup

Description

Creamy chicken soup with a mild peanut butter and coconut taste.

Ingredients

  • 1.3 litres water
  • 2 cubes of chicken bouillon
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp. peanut butter
  • 400g chicken thigh fillet
  • 400g leek (cut into thin pieces)
  • 2x "Shanghai" bok choy
  • 180g ramen noodles
  • 1 red pepper

Steps

  1. Add the water, chicken bouillon and peanut butter into a pan and bring it to a boil all while stirring. Add the chicken and the leek and leave it to gently boil for about 10 minutes. Add salt as well as pepper if preferred.
  2. Cut the bok choy into thin pieces en add the white part of the bok choy to the soup, together with the noodles, boil for 5 more minutes.
  3. Remove the stem from the pepper and cut the pepper into rings (if you're not a fan of spicy food, remove the seeds). Serve the soup in bowls and add the green part of the bok choy as well as the pepper.
